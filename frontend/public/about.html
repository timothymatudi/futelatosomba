About futelatosomba

Our Mission

futelatosomba is dedicated to revolutionizing the property market in the Democratic Republic of Congo. We believe that finding your dream home or investment property should be accessible, transparent, and straightforward.

Our platform connects property seekers with verified listings across Kinshasa, Lubumbashi, Goma, and beyond, ensuring that every transaction is secure and every customer is satisfied.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, futelatosomba emerged from a simple observation: the DRC property market needed a modern, reliable platform that serves both local residents and international investors.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of users, from first-time homebuyers to seasoned real estate investors, all looking for their perfect property in one of Africa's most dynamic markets.

Our Values

  • Transparency: Every listing is verified and accurate
  • Community: We give back through educational programs for children
  • Innovation: Leveraging technology to improve property search
  • Trust: Building lasting relationships with our users
  • Accessibility: Making property ownership achievable for all
